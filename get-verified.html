Get Your Carrier Credential

Submit your documents below to join the Midnight Loadboard verified carrier network. Once approved, you'll receive a Trucker Credential Token (TCT) — a permanent digital credential on the Cardano blockchain that proves your CDL, insurance, and FMCSA authority to every shipper in the network.

Verification is free. No fees. No subscriptions.

Fill out the form below with your information and paste links to your documents. We'll review everything within 24-48 hours.

3

Receive Your TCT

Once verified, your Trucker Credential Token is minted and sent directly to your wallet. You're now in the network and can claim loads.

4

Get Paid Fast

TCT carriers on Midnight Loadboard receive payment within 7 days of delivery — guaranteed. No NET30, no factoring companies, no chasing brokers. Payments can be settled in USDM stablecoin via GameChanger Wallet.
