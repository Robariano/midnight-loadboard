How to Mint a Document
1

Get Your Document Into PDF Form

GameChanger's minting tool requires a PDF file. If your document isn't already a PDF, use a free converter like freeconvert.com — upload your file, choose PDF as the output, then download the converted file.

2

Open GameChanger Wallet

Go to gamechanger.finance and open your wallet. Creating one is free and takes about 2 minutes. You'll need a small amount of ADA to cover the minting fee (usually less than $0.50).

3

Use the NFT Minting Tool

In GameChanger, go to Tools → Mint NFT. Upload your PDF, give it a name (e.g. "BOL — July 18 — Durango to Albuquerque"), and mint. The document gets stored on IPFS and a permanent token is sent to your wallet.

4

Save Your IPFS Link

After minting, GameChanger gives you a permanent IPFS link to your document. Save it — you can share it with brokers or shippers who need to verify the delivery. It never expires.
